Features

Everything you need to
stand out in the job hunt

A five-step AI agent does the heavy lifting — from researching the company to crafting every line of your application.

🌐
Live Company Research
The agent fetches the company website in real time, extracting mission, culture, and values to personalize every output.
🔍
Deep JD Analysis
Extracts required skills, experience level, key responsibilities, and cultural signals from any job description.
✏️
ATS-Optimized Resume
Every bullet is rewritten with job-relevant keywords, strong action verbs, and quantifiable impact. Includes an ATS match score.
📝
Personalized Cover Letter
A complete 3–4 paragraph cover letter tailored to the role and company, with a ready-to-use email subject line.
💬
Smart Interview Questions
6–8 insightful questions to ask the interviewer, categorized by topic and explained with why they signal genuine interest.
📄
PDF Upload, No Server
Drag-and-drop your PDF resume. Text is extracted entirely in the browser — your file never leaves your device.
